About MGIT Campus Assistant

MGIT Campus Assistant is your personal voice assistant designed to help students, faculty, and staff at Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Technology (MGIT) get the information they need quickly and efficiently.

The assistant can help with:

  • Providing campus information
  • Assisting with navigation
  • Giving important contact details
  • Handling various student queries

This project aims to improve the user experience on campus by making essential information accessible through voice commands, creating a seamless and vibrant interaction with campus resources.
